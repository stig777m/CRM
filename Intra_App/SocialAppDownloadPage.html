How to Download?

For Android Devices:

  1. Click on the "Download for Android" Button.
  2. The Download will commence automatically.
  3. Once the application has been successfully downloaded, click on "Install" to initiate the installation.
  4. If prompted, grant permission for installation from unknown sources by selecting "Yes" in the settings, and proceed with the installation process.
  5. If the download doesn't start automatically, You can Click Here.
